Week 2!

Workshop Activity 1: Crazy Eights

In this workshop activity, we were tasked with designing 8 ideas for our workbook in 8 minutes— 1 idea x 1 minute x 8 minutes. We completed 8 quick visualisation
sketches of our ideas, which were then shared with the class through a pinup.
I was predominantly inspired by the idea that imagery could act as a button, and wanted to play with that idea in my workbook design. I love the interactivity and
playfulness enabled by this idea, and I think having unsuspecting buttons would be a fun and engaging way to navigate through folders in my workbook.

My favourite ideation sketch was the idea of a digital garden, where the flowers would act as buttons that lead to different pages. I also love the metaphorical aspect—
flowers grow and evolve over time, in synonymity with how my workbook is always in evolution. Overall, I think the concept of an everblossoming digital garden is so
beautiful, and it excites me to further explore this idea.
