Workshop Activity 1: "Human Computer in your Face"- Gesture Activity

This was the first workshop activity we completed in class! Working in a group of 3, we designed two hand gestures/ speculative interfaces that enable human-nonhuman
interactivityâ€” the "Bunny" and "Eye-wipe" gesture.
The "Bunny" gesture works as a positive gesticulation, functioning as a complimentary hand motion that signals excitement and elation. In contrast, the "Eye-wipe"
gesture prompts an action. When this gesture is performed, it prompts the coffee machine to power on and start making coffee. Ultimately, we found the "Eye-wipe"
gesture was the more successful idea!

Workshop Activity 2: TAG! [HTML tag relay]

In this activity we tried to construct a webpage of our choice using paper tags. Through this activity, we learnt the basic elements and components of an HTML page.
